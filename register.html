One Click Feed Validation for Firefox

If you are...

  • A frequent user of Firefox for browsing the web.
  • An occasional user of the FeedValidator.
  • An infrequent user of Firefox's feature for subscribing to feeds.

... then you might find it useful to register the FeedValidator as a Feed Reader. Simply follow the instructions at the top of this page, and the feed validator will only be one click away. Once registered, you can validate any feed merely by subscribing to it.

Such subscriptions are not persistent; if you wish to revalidate a feed, simply subscribe again.
